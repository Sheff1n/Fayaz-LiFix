What We Offer

At LiFix, we offer a wide range of services tailored to meet your individual rehabilitation and wellness needs.

Lifestyle Diabetic Physiotherapy

Our Lifestyle Diabetic Physiotherapy program combines targeted exercises and lifestyle modifications to help manage diabetes, improve mobility, and enhance overall health, empowering you to take control of your well-being naturally.

Dry Needle

Dry needling therapy is an innovative, highly effective treatment designed to relieve muscle pain and tension. By using thin, sterile needles to target trigger points or tight bands of muscle, this therapy promotes natural healing, improves mobility, and reduces discomfort.

Manual Therapy

Manual therapy is a hands-on treatment that relieves pain, improves mobility, and restores function through targeted techniques like joint mobilization and muscle stretching.

Giant Training Unit

An innovative program that boosts strength and endurance through high-intensity workouts. Tailored for all fitness levels, it combines functional movements with specialized equipment in a supportive environment.

Traction Therapy

A specialized treatment that alleviates pain and improves mobility by gently stretching the spine and relieving pressure on the discs and nerves. Experience relief and enhanced flexibility with traction therapy, designed to promote healing and restore balance to your body!

Exercise Therapy Unit

Our Exercise Therapy Unit offers personalized, guided workouts designed to improve strength, flexibility, and overall health, helping you feel stronger and more energized every day!

Mirror Therapy

Mirror therapy is a breakthrough treatment that uses the power of reflection to retrain your brain, helping to reduce pain and improve movement in affected limbs, offering a unique path to recovery.

Electro Therapy

Electrotherapy uses gentle electrical impulses to reduce pain, improve circulation, and accelerate healing, offering a highly effective and non-invasive treatment for muscle and nerve issues.

Sensory Integration Unit

Our Sensory Integration Unit provides specialized therapy to help improve how the brain processes sensory information, enhancing coordination, focus, and overall daily functioning for individuals with sensory challenges.
